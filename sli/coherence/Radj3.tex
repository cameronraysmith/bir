\begin{block}{transitivity of sieves gives an extended coequalizer}
Given the transitivity of $S(L_0)$, there is an epimorphic family represented by $\{ N_0^{ij} \rightarrow M_0^{'i} \times_{L_0} M_0^i \}_{ij}$ that we can precompose with the coequalizer above yielding
\begin{displaymath}
\xymatrix{
\coprod\limits_{i,j} N_0^{ij}
\ar@<1ex>[r]^-{\alpha} \ar@<-1ex>[r]_-{\beta}
&
\coprod\limits_{g_i \in S(L_0)} M_0^i
\ar[r]^-{b_{S(L_0)}}
&
L_0
}
\end{displaymath}
\end{block}
There is a commutative diagram in $\mathcal{L}_0$ of the following form
\begin{displaymath}
\xymatrix{
N_0^{ij} \ar[r]^-{h} \ar[d]_{k} & M_0^i \ar[d]^{g_i} \\
M_0^{'i} \ar[r]_-{g'_i} & L_0
}
\end{displaymath}
for each $L_0 \in \Ob(\mathcal{L}_0)$ ($\alpha$ is associated to $h$ and $\beta$ is associated to $k$).
\end{frame}
