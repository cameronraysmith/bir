\begin{frame}
Given a functor $A: \mathcal{L}_0 \rightarrow \mathcal{L}_1$, recall the adjunction $L_A \dashv R_A$ between the category of presheaves on the local category $\mathcal{L}_0$ and the global category $\mathcal{L}_1$:
$$
L_A: \textit{Sets}^{\mathcal{L}_0^{opp}} \leftrightarrows \mathcal{L}_1 :R_A.
$$
\end{frame}
