\begin{block}{(co)unit isomorphisms}
The counit, $\epsilon$, and the unit $\eta$, are both isomorphisms for the adjunction $L_A \dashv R_A$ upon restriction from $Sets^{\mathcal{L}_0^{opp}}$ to sheaves on the site defined by $J$ in terms of epimorphic families of morphisms in $\mathcal{L}_1$ for $\mathcal{L}_0$ written as $Sh(\mathcal{L}_0,J)$ 
\begin{eqnarray*}
\eta &:& 1_{Sh(\mathcal{L}_0,J)} \cong R_A \circ L_A,\\
\epsilon &:& L_A \circ R_A \cong 1_{\mathcal{L}_1}.
\end{eqnarray*}
\end{block}
\begin{block}{equivalence of categories}
We thus have an equivalence of categories between the category of local representations of biological information and that of global representations
$$
Sh(\mathcal{L}_0,J) \cong \mathcal{L}_1.
$$
\end{block}
\end{frame}
