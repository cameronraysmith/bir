\begin{frame}
The functor $L$ is unique for a given $A$ and all functors $A$ from a small category $\mathcal{L}_0$ to a cocomplete category $\mathcal{L}_1$ factor through the Yoneda embedding $PSh$. 
\begin{block}{free cocompletion / Yoneda extension}
The Yoneda embedding is thus the universal method of appending colimits to a small category as suggested by the diagram
$$
\xymatrix{
& \mathcal{L}_1 & \\
\mathcal{L}_0 \ar[ru]^{A} \ar[rr]_{PSh} & & \textit{Sets}^{\mathcal{L}_0^{opp}} \ar@{-->}[lu]_{L}
}
$$
which commutes uniquely in $L$ for a given $A$.
\end{block}
\end{frame}
