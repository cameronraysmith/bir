\begin{block}{category of elements}
\begin{enumerate}
\item The objects of $\int_{\cC} P$ are pairs $(C,x)$ where $C \in \Ob(\cC)$ and $x \in PC$.
\item The morphisms are triples $(g,(C',x'),(C,x))$ where $g:(C',x') \rightarrow (C,x)$ derives from $g: C' \rightarrow C \in \Mor(\cC)$ and these satisfy the condition
$$
P(g)(x)=x'.
$$
\end{enumerate}
\end{block}

There is also a projection functor that recovers structure in $\cC$ from the category of elements of $P$
$$
\pi : \int_{\cC} P \rightarrow \cC
$$
defined on objects and morphisms of the category of elements of $P$ respectively as $\pi(C,x)=C$ and $\pi (g,(C',x'),(C,x)) = g$.
